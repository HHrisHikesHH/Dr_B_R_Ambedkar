# Dr. B. R. Ambedkar — Architect of a Just India

## About This Page

This is a beautiful educational webpage about the life and work of **Dr. Bhimrao Ramji Ambedkar**.  

It explains in simple and respectful language his important contributions to India — as the chief architect of the Indian Constitution, a great scholar, a fighter for Dalit rights, women’s rights, and social justice.
